About this project

Designed by fwstudio.

Delishfind is a full front-end app. It was built with HTML5, SASS, CSS, and Vanilla Javascript. Its search functionality is powered by travel advisor rest api, which provides information about restaurant all around the world. The app will let you search for restaurants within the city you are in and will allow you to visualize the exact location of every restaurant through real-time maps implemented with the leaftlet JS library.
